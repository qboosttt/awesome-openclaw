## 📝 About awesome-openclaw

awesome-openclaw is a simple and reliable program designed to help you control claw machines or robotic claws through your computer. Whether you want to operate a claw toy at home or manage a claw-based device for fun or work, this app makes the process straightforward with a user-friendly interface.

This application supports popular operating systems like Windows and Mac. It offers smooth control features, responsive feedback, and provides clear status updates while you operate your claw machine.

## 💻 System Requirements

Before you download and run awesome-openclaw, make sure your computer meets these minimum requirements:

- Operating System: Windows 10 or newer, or macOS 10.13 (High Sierra) or newer
- Processor: Intel or AMD processor at 1.6 GHz or higher
- RAM: 4 GB or more
- Storage: At least 100 MB free space
- Internet connection: Required for download and occasional updates
- USB or Bluetooth port: If your claw device connects via these methods

If you are unsure about your system specs, you can find them in your computer’s "Settings" or "About" section.

## 🛠 Troubleshooting Tips

If you encounter problems, try these solutions:

- **App won’t open:** Restart your computer and try again.
- **Device not detected:** Check that the claw is properly plugged in or paired via Bluetooth.
- **Controls not working:** Close the app and reopen. Ensure you selected the correct device.
- **Download won’t start:** Use a different web browser or check your internet connection.
- **Installation fails:** Ensure you have enough storage space and try running the installer as administrator (Windows).

If these tips don’t help, check the Issues section on the project page or contact support.
